Good jobs require a good education, and too many people face systematic barriers that are preventing them from getting the college experience that they deserve. The harsh truth is that US colleges are businesses, and student loans pay the bills. According to the most recent figures from the Project on Student Debt, seven out of ten college graduates leave school with loan debt. A decade ago, their average debt level was around $17,000 â€” but, as of last year, that figure had climbed close to $30,000, directly impacting 37 million student borrowers and creating a trillion-dollar problem that affects just about everyone in the nation.

Many university courses have gone online now due to the ongoing health crisis, and UZoom is trying to make the best of a bad situation by making good education accessible to anyone who wants to learn, no matter where they're from, how old they are, or what they're able to afford. We believe that if you're self-motivated and curious, nothing should come in the way of stopping you. It is somewhat of a social experiment - if this website is abused by bored trolls who aim to disrupt virtual classrooms, it will be taken down; however, more than anything, we hope that it will start a much needed conversation on how to give disadvantaged people the tools to improve their lives.
